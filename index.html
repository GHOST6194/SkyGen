About SkyGen

A Skygen is a type of Minecraft server game mode inspired by Skyblock but with a unique twist. In a Skygen server, players start on a small floating island, similar to Skyblock, but instead of gathering resources traditionally, they collect materials from specialized generators on their island. Hereâ€™s how it works:

Generators: These are blocks that automatically generate resources like ores, blocks, or other valuable items over time. Players can stand on or near these generators to collect materials passively.

Progression: The goal in Skygen is often to upgrade generators, tools, and island structures to gather resources faster and expand the island.

PvP and Economy: Some Skygen servers add PvP areas or an in-game economy where players can sell or trade resources, adding competitive or cooperative elements to the gameplay.

Skygen is popular because it combines resource management and island expansion with a more automated and relaxed gameplay style compared to traditional Skyblock.
