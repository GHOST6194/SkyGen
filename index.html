About SkyGen

SkyGen is a unique Minecraft experience designed for players to generate resources, sell them for in-game currency, and progress through a series of features and challenges. There are shops for buying and selling, effect and kit shops, and fun activities like parkours, mazes, and mini-games. Players can use their earned currency to upgrade their experience and access special areas like VIP plots, pet shops, and more!
